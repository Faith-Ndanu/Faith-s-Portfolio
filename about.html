About Me

Faith Munyao

Software Developer

Raised in Kenya's slums, Faith witnessed the struggles with overflowing trash, a breeding ground for disease, and a constant threat to health. Seeing no one deserved such a life, Faith aims to revolutionize waste management using her software skills. She strives to contribute to a sustainable and environmentally conscious future for her community by contributing to solutions like smart bin sensors and waste-to-energy conversion.
